About
The index I built for myself.
Now it's yours.

I've been collecting design resources for years. Not casually. Going down a rabbit hole of type foundries at 12am on a random Tuesday. Studying the greats, dissecting not just what I like, but why. Analyzing. Trying to elevate my taste and ultimately my work every single day. That obsession never really turned off.

Design Index is what came out of it. The Google Sheets weren't enough. I've built plenty of makeshift resource lists to share with designers, but never a tightly curated index until now.

This is what I wish someone had handed me when I first started in graphic design. Ten years later, this is the index I still use every day. I made it for myself, but I wanted to share it with you. Every link earns its spot. If it hasn't made me better, I'm not putting it in front of you.

This is an ongoing project. I will add, delete, and modify the index every month. I'm endlessly curious. I know I can learn from anyone. I'm obsessed with finding the best stuff out there. And my promise to myself and to you is I won't lose that hunger.

I'm also just one person. I know I don't see it all. And I know you have incredible resources on hand that aren't in here yet. If you're up for it, I'd love for this to be a collaborative project. Something living, active, and built by the graphic design community.

If you have a resource that deserves a spot, please submit it here.

If you want to follow along as this grows, I send out a free weekly email called The Creative Mix. New resources, design finds, and whatever I'm currently obsessing over. It's for the designer who stays curious, keeps growing, and wants to be the most equipped person in the room. Subscribe here.
